Best Easy Omelette Recipe

This quick and easy omelette is the perfect solution for busy mornings or lazy brunches. With just a few ingredients and simple steps, you’ll have a fluffy, cheesy omelette on your plate in minutes.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: 5 minutes
  • Cook: 5 minutes
  • Total: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ tbsp olive oil
  • 3 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • ½ cup grated cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  2. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk until well mixed.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and let it spread evenly.
  4. Use a spatula to gently stir and prevent sticking.
  5. Let the omelette cook undisturbed for about 3 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle grated cheese evenly over the top.
  7. Fold one half of the omelette over the other.
  8. Slide onto a plate and let the residual heat finish cooking.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
